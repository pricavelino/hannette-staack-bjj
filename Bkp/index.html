About the Champion

More Than a Fighter.
A Movement.

Hannette Staack is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt, co-founder of Brazil-021 School of Jiu-Jitsu, and one of the greatest competitors in the sport's history. With seven world titles and an ADCC championship, her legacy on the mats is unmatched.

Beyond her achievements, Hannette is recognized as an exceptional teacher and a key figure in strengthening the female presence in Jiu-Jitsu — inspiring a new generation of athletes around the world.
